Ting-Wei Li

📍 Champaign, IL, USA. 📧 twli AT illinois DOT edu.

I’m Ting-Wei (Tim) Li (李廷威), an incoming Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science @ University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My supervisor is Prof. Hanghang Tong.

My research interest lies in graph machine learning, trustworthy machine learning and any intersection of the above two areas. For applications, I am particually interested in social network analysis and interactions between LLMs and Knowledge Graphs.

Before joining UIUC, I received my master’s degree from ECE @ University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and bachelor’s degree from EE @ National Taiwan University. I also work extensively with Prof. Qiaozhu Mei, Prof. Jiaqi Ma and Prof. Yao Ma.
